Data Modernization

Data modernization refers to the process of transforming and upgrading an organization's data infrastructure, systems, and practices to keep up with the evolving demands of the digital age. It involves adopting modern technologies, methodologies, and tools to effectively collect, store, manage, analyze, and leverage data for decision-making and operational efficiency.
